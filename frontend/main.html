Jon Conway's Game of Life

Controls

Orange cells are alive and blue cells are not (and anything not within the grid is assumed unpopulated). Choose a preset configuration of live cells from the dropdown menu or create a new one by clicking the cell grid. The 'Start' button advances (or pauses) the game, and the rules (shown below) are applied at each advancing step.

Rules

For a space that is 'populated':
  • Each cell with one or no neighbors dies, as if by solitude.
  • Each cell with four or more neighbors dies, as if by overpopulation.
  • Each cell with two or three neighbors survives.
For a space that is 'empty' or 'unpopulated'
  • Each cell with three neighbors becomes populated.
